# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Football fans aged 18–40 watching FIFA World Cup 2026 matches — at home, in a bar, or in the stadium. They need bathroom breaks and snack runs but are terrified of missing a goal. They're glued to their phones during matches anyway.

## Product Purpose

NeverMiss tells fans when it's safe to step away from a match — in real-time, based on live match intensity. The landing page exists to drive installs of the PWA at app.nevermiss.click. Success: visitor lands, immediately recognises their own problem, taps "Open App" within seconds.

## Brand Personality

Playful · Sharp · Confident

Voice is irreverent and self-aware — the kind of mate who thought of an idea everyone wishes they'd had. Humor is grounded in real fan behavior (bathroom breaks, snack runs, "rizzing them up"). The tone never winks too hard; it lands the joke and moves on.

## Anti-references

- **FIFA official / corporate**: over-produced, sponsor-heavy, zero personality. No sponsor-blue, no trophy renders, no "The Beautiful Game" language.
- **SaaS landing page**: "10x your match experience" energy, hero metrics, gradient text, numbered-section eyebrows, cream-colored parchment backgrounds.
- **Hypebeast streetwear**: all-caps stacking, aggressive drop-shadows, maximalist decoration, shouting hierarchy.

## Design Principles

1. **Self-aware absurdity** — the premise (bathroom break app) is inherently funny. Lean into it; don't sanitize the copy for a broader audience.
2. **Relief, not hype** — the emotional goal is recognition ("finally, someone built this"), not amazement. Visuals and copy should feel like a solution found, not a product sold.
3. **Sharp restraint** — Confident means the page doesn't need to over-explain or over-decorate. One CTA, clear hierarchy, no filler sections.
4. **Fan-made feel** — every design decision should feel like it came from a fan, not a product team. Specificity over polish.
5. **Dark by nature** — fans watch in dim rooms, on phones with brightness down. The dark theme isn't aesthetic posturing; it's the right physical scene.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG target. Minimum bar: body text readable at normal screen brightness, tap targets at least 44px, semantic HTML for screen readers. Reduced motion alternative for any animations.
